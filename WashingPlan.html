Washing Plan

Choose Your Plan

Basic cleaning

Your car will be lathered with a foam-based cleaner, rinsed, and dried. These types of washes can be automated (meaning that brushes or wipes are used to aid in the cleansing process) or they may be touchless (meaning that only the cleansing, rinsing, and drying agents touch your vehicle).

Premium cleaning

Premium wash engages proper detailing tools, products and techniques to get the job done. The extra mile of cleaning areas that are usually left untouched or unseen during regular washes to maintain the overall cleanliness of the entire car. No cross-contamination.

Complex cleaning

Complex car wash is a whole range of services, combined into one procedure. It includes washing the bodywork, rugs and thresholds, cleaning the interior and trunk, cleaning the windows from the inside with special chemicals, cleaning the ventilation holes and blowing.
