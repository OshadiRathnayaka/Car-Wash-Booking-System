WHAT WE DO ?



Exterior Washing

The first of the basic detailing procedures is a prep wash of the exterior. This way you remove dirt, dust, and other loose contaminants. Next comes paint cleaning and rejuvenation to make the car surface look great.


Interior Washing

Interior detailing is about cleaning the inner parts of a vehicle: vacuuming, wiping and conditioning leathers and vinyl, carbon fiber plastics and natural fibers.


Vacuum Cleaning

A car vacuum cleaner makes it simple to remove all the unsanitary dirt and hidden trash from in between the seats. The greatest cleaners are handheld models because of their portability, small size, and compact design.


Seats Washing

Use a vinegar mixture. Mix a cup of vinegar, a few drops of dish soap and about a gallon of hot water in a bucket. Then dab the mixture into the stain and use a brush to scrub it out of the seat, use clean water to rinse the seat off. Use laundry detergent.


Window Wiping

Take a microfiber rag and wipe the window from top to bottom. Next, use a microfiber rag and isopropyl alcohol to clean and degrease the window. Spray a generous amount of cleaning solution on microfiber rag.


Wet Cleaning

With wet cleaning, water and detergent are added to a specially made, computer-controlled machine along with the garments. Clothing can be agitated extremely gently or dried at a very specific temperature, allowing cleaners to completely customize the wet cleaning process for each item.


Oil Changing

An oil change is the process of removing and replacing the viscous fluid- otherwise known as oil- that runs through an internal combustion engine


Brake Repairing

A brake repair is when certain mechanical parts like your car's brake pads and shoes, calipers, and rotors need repair or replacement.
